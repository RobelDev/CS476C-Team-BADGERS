Team Badgers

Skills/Knowledge Badging System

Welcome to Team Badger's Computer Science Capstone Landing Page. As a team, we have worked together to develop an internal social media to recognize skills and hard work for the employees at State Farm Insurance. With the use of a badging system, we have brought back an aspect of a social working environment that has been lost due to the pandemic.
